The Kickstarter United Oral History

In 2020, one of our former organizers,Clarissa Redwine, teamed up with NYU's Engelberg Center to compile an oral history of our union from genesis all the way to the vote win. You can also search for "Engelberg Center Live!" in your podcast app of choice to find these episodes and more.
